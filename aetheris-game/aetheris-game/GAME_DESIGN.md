# Game Design Reference

This game is based on the complete design document: `../RPS-RPG-Design-Document.md`

## Quick Reference

### Characters
- **Kael** (Male): Paper-aligned (60%), strategic and adaptable
- **Lyra** (Female): Scissors-aligned (60%), precise and analytical

### Combat System
- Rock beats Scissors
- Scissors beats Paper
- Paper beats Rock
- Alignment bonuses: +10% damage per 10% affinity
- Symbol Break: Ultimate move available at 100 Resolve or 3 consecutive wins

### World Regions
- **Rock Dominion**: Mountains, mines, defensive culture
- **Paper Dominion**: Libraries, floating cities, strategic culture
- **Scissor Dominion**: Blade forests, precision, speed culture
- **Crosspoint**: Neutral hub where all factions meet

### Story Structure
- **Act I**: The Cracked Balance - Introduction and tutorial
- **Act II**: Crossing the Dominions - Explore all three regions
- **Act III**: The Hidden Symbol - Final confrontation
